Blueprints

What is Blueprints?

You could think of Blueprints as a UI framework, primarily focused on look and feel, or in other words - CSS. It's a long living styleguide that can evolve over time and empower developers to build application pages quickly whilst conforming with a consistent branding that is agreed and defined.

How is it built?

Blueprints is essentially built on top of Bootstrap 3. This allows us to use solid foundations (a range of components, grid system and documentation) that almost every developer is familiar with, but adopt it to our organisation needs.
